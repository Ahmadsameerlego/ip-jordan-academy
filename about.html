About Jordan IB Network

Supporting the International Baccalaureate community in Jordan

Our Mission

Jordan IB Network is a platform dedicated to supporting the International Baccalaureate IB community in Jordan. We connect students and parents with qualified IB certified tutors, help schools enroll top IB educators, and offer training programs for teachers who want to enter the IB system.

Our mission is to create a comprehensive ecosystem that supports the growth and success of IB education in Jordan. We believe that every student deserves access to quality IB education, and every teacher deserves the opportunity to develop their skills in this prestigious program.

Through our network, we facilitate meaningful connections between students, parents, teachers, and schools, ensuring that the IB community in Jordan thrives and continues to produce exceptional graduates.

What Makes Us Unique

IB-CERTIFIED ONLY

We exclusively work with IB-certified teachers who have undergone rigorous training and meet the highest standards of the International Baccalaureate program.

SCHOOL & STUDENT SUPPORT

We provide comprehensive support for both schools looking to enhance their IB programs and students seeking guidance throughout their IB journey.

PATHWAY FOR TEACHERS

We offer clear pathways for teachers to become IB-certified, including training programs, mentorship, and career development opportunities.

Our Values

Excellence

We maintain the highest standards of quality in everything we do, ensuring that our network represents the best of IB education.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of community and work together with all stakeholders to strengthen the IB ecosystem in Jordan.

Innovation

We continuously seek new ways to improve and expand our services, embracing modern educational practices and technologies.

Passion

We are passionate about education and committed to making a positive impact on the lives of students and teachers.
