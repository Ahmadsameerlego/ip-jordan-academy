About Jordan IB Network

Jordan IB Network is a platform dedicated to supporting the International Baccalaureate IB community in Jordan. We connect students and parents with qualified IB certified tutors, help schools enroll top IB educators, and offer training programs for teachers who want to enter the IB system.
