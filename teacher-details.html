About Sarah

Sarah is a highly qualified IB Mathematics and Physics teacher with over 8 years of experience in international education. She holds a Master's degree in Mathematics Education and is certified in both IB Mathematics HL/SL and Physics HL/SL.

Sarah has successfully guided hundreds of students through their IB journey, with a 95% pass rate and many students achieving top scores of 6 and 7. Her teaching approach focuses on building strong conceptual understanding while developing problem-solving skills.

She specializes in:

  • IB Mathematics Analysis and Approaches (HL/SL)
  • IB Mathematics Applications and Interpretation (HL/SL)
  • IB Physics (HL/SL)
  • Internal Assessment guidance
  • Extended Essay supervision
